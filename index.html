Welcome to Neurowit professional Services---Moe Rahnema

Moe Rahnema is a senior telecommunications systems and network engineering consultant with extensive practical experience across radio access networks/RF, IP/core architecture, and satellite communications systems

With more than 15 years of extensive experience in the wireless telecommunications industry, I combine deep practical expertise with a strong theoretical foundation across 4G, 3G/UMTS, GSM, GPRS, and EDGE technologies. Throughout my career, I have partnered with leading international mobile operators and equipment vendors, serving in senior key technical consulting positions, and project coordination roles. My experience spans both the radio network and core network planning and expansion, network architecture and solution design, KPI definition and optimization, vendor product evaluation and selection, and performance measurement and benchmarking for both voice and data services

In addition, I have also several years of proven experience in R@ D from my prior employee positions in the US in the design and development of signal detection and processing algorithms for satellite communication systems, design and development of voiceband modems, and in research and development of high speed packet switching systems and networks.

In recent years, I have engaged in independent research and study of AI algorithms and their application for data analysis and trending with case studies in telecommunication and other data analytic areas
