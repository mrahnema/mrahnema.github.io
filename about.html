About Me

I bring over three decades of experience across the full evolution of modern telecommunications systems—from early ATM switching and voiceband modem design, through satellite communications and IP network architecture, to large-scale mobile network planning, performance analysis and optimization from 2G to 4G /LTE technologies
I have worked with global organizations including major equipment vendors, operators, and satellite communication systems, building, leading, mentoring and managing technical teams and projects, designing network architectures, and delivering optimization strategies that significantly improved network performance, capacity, and service quality
My work consistently bridges deep technical engineering, algorithmic/system design, and executive-level advisory, enabling operators to improve performance while reducing cost and accelerating deployment cycles
In addition, I have working knowledge of AI and ML algorithms, their applications in the field of telecommunication and generally data analytics, and with some practical hands-on experience in Python programming and the related libraries
I provide independent consulting and advisory services to mobile network operators, vendors, Consulting organizations, and data analytic industries
